Peregrine Falcon

The peregrine falcon is the fastest animal on the planet. In a 'stoop' they can reach up to speeds of 290 km/h.

  • Scientific name: Falco Peregrinus
  • Size: 40-46 cm with wingspan of 95-110 cm
  • Range: Europe, Asia, North and South America
  • Type: Bird

The peregrine falcon is the best hunter of the falcon family. It hunts in wild uplands and rocky coasts, scanning the landscape for prey with its exceptional eyesight. Their eyes are larger and heavier than human eyes and they can spot prey on the ground from a great height of 300m.
